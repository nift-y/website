Our mission

ASL Junction strives to connect family and friends inside and out of the ASL Community. We desire to create and support an integrated community of signers for those we love. We encourage communication through sharing educational and social activities, in and out of the deaf community.

Language First

90% of children who are deaf are raised by hearing parents. Less than 40% of families with school aged deaf children use sign language at home according to a study from Gallaudet University.

Community

As parents we have seen our deaf children isolated in hearing communities, and we have rejoiced when a child took time to learn to learn a sign. We are here to broaden playground signers.


We share ASL to everyone - for their first or second language.
